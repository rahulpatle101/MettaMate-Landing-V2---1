1. Core Identity & Purpose

  • Product: MettaMate
  • Concept: A community-driven digital platform (social network + ecosystem) specifically designed for Ambedkarites (followers of Dr. B.R. Ambedkar).
  • Primary Value Prop: Providing a dignified, safe space for connection, healing, and growth where members do not have to "translate" their history or justify their existence.
  • Target Audience: Individuals seeking deep community connection, professional mentorship, and safety from caste-based discrimination found on other platforms.

2. Design System & Aesthetics

  • Visual Tone: Warm, intellectual, calm, and grounded. It avoids "tech startup" coldness in favor of "community wellness."
  • Color Palette:
    • Primary: Bhim Blue (#2A4B9B) (Historically significant) & Dark Blue (#1A2F63).
    • Secondary/Accent: Warm Gold (#D4A056) & Deep Gold (#B8863D).
    • Background: Paper/Off-White (#F9F7F2) to mimic the feel of books/literature.
  • Typography:
    • Headings: Playfair Display (Serif) – Evokes history, literature, and dignity.
    • Body: Inter (Sans-Serif) – Clean, modern, readable.
  • UI Components:
    • Rounded corners (rounded-3xl) for a soft, organic feel.
    • Glassmorphism (backdrop blur) used on overlays to suggest depth.
    • Animations: reveal-on-scroll (fade-up), pulse-soft (breathing effect), and stagger delays. Motion is slow and human-paced, not flashy.

3. Site Structure (Section by Section)

  1. Navigation: Sticky top bar. Glass-effect on scroll. Links: Connect, Grow, Support, Safety. CTA: "Join Now."
  2. Hero Section:
    • Visual: Full-screen background image with a warm overlay.
    • Copy: "Where you simply belong." Emphasis on dignity and shared experience.
    • Social Proof: "15,000+ Members Online."
  3. Showing Up (Carousel):
    • Horizontal scrollable cards depicting community moments (Joyful Gatherings, Quiet Reflection).
    • Interaction: Snap-scroll carousel with hover effects revealing captions.
  4. Events (Connect):
    • Horizontal scroll of specific cultural/community events (e.g., Dhamma Chakra Day, Healing Circles).
    • Design: Cards with date badges and background images.
  5. Manifesto (Our Story):
    • Layout: Split screen (Image + Text).
    • Key Message: "You’ve explained enough." Addresses the fatigue of code-switching and offers a space of rest.
    • Quote: Dr. B.R. Ambedkar on the cultivation of the mind.
  6. Bridge Section (Value Grid):
    • 4-column icon grid highlighting practical benefits: Connections, Career, Mentorship, Initiatives.
  7. Grow Section (Bento Grid):
    • Layout: Asymmetrical grid (Bento box style).
    • Content: Mentorship (large card), Career Hub, Community Stories, and Relationships.
  8. Voices (Testimonials):
    • Design: Cards on a clean background.
    • Content: Emotional testimonials focusing on "taking off the armor" and "safety."
  9. Impact Section:
    • Visual: Solid Blue background.
    • Content: High-level metrics (Lives Connected, Cities Active).
  10. Global Gathering (Map):
    • Visual: Abstract, borderless SVG world map.
    • Interaction: Pulsing nodes (cities like Toronto, London, Mumbai) showing global reach without borders.
  11. Support Section (Economy):
    • Focus: "Community Economy" / Circular Wealth.
    • Cards: Business, Nonprofit, Mutual Aid, Collective.
  12. Safety Section:
    • Visual: Dark Blue background with subtle texture.
    • Message: "Safety is our love language." Highlights human verification and zero tolerance for hate/casteism.
  13. FAQ & Footer: Standard accordion FAQ and a "Come Home" final CTA footer with newsletter signup.

4. Content Tone Guidelines for AI Variations

When creating variations, the AI should adhere to these tonal rules:

  • Do: Use words like Dignity, Roots, Cultivation, Kinship, Ecosystem, Sanctuary, Ancestors, Future.
  • Don't: Use aggressive marketing language like Disrupt, Crush, Exclusive Access, Hype, FOMO.
  • Emotion: The site should feel like a deep breath of relief. It is protective yet progressive.

5. Technical Stack Context

  • Framework: React (Functional Components).
  • Styling: Tailwind CSS (via CDN) + Custom CSS injected via styles constant.
  • Icons: lucide-react.
  • State Management: useState, useEffect, useRef (primarily for scroll interactions).
